Our Mission

Anubhav began its journey during the devastating COVID-19 outbreak. When the world was in lockdown, we saw families in Namkhana and Sundarbans starving for basic needs. We launched back-to-back relief projects for 100 households, providing food, sanitary pads, and baby food. That commitment to humanity drives us today across West Bengal.

Our Impact

Where Anubhav Began

The Sundarbans isn't just a project location for us; it is the birthplace of Anubhav. Our journey began here during the height of the COVID-19 outbreak, when the region was paralyzed by lockdowns. Witnessing families in Namkhana and the deep interior struggle for basic needs, we launched our first-ever relief drives to help over 100 households.

1. Peniabhnga - Festive Joy

Durga Puja 2024 | Collab: Mrs. Sapna Midya

On the auspicious eve of Durga Puja, we celebrated with the SABAR tribal group. We distributed 60 new clothes, learning kits, cakes, and biscuits to children of different age groups. We also arranged a community lunch for 100 people.

2. Gopiballavpur - Winter Relief

29th Dec 2024 | Collab: Subarnaseva

We carried out a challenging project in the remote village of Gopiballavpur to aid tribal families during the harsh winter. We donated 60 blankets to help them battle the cold. Our work was recognized by the NGO "Subarnaseva".

1. Pabra Village

29th Dec 2024 | Winter Relief

As temperatures plummeted in rural Bengal, our team mobilized to Pabra village with a singular mission: to shield the vulnerable. On December 29th, 2024, we distributed 50 thick blankets to the elderly and destitute families. This Winter Relief drive was a critical intervention to prevent cold-related illnesses and provide warmth and comfort during the harshest nights of the year.

2. Gangajalghati

Sept 28, 2024 | Durga Puja

On the auspicious eve of Durga Puja, we turned a simple distribution drive into a celebration of childhood. We reached out to 60 children in Gangajalghati who would otherwise go without new clothes for the festival.

Development Drives

In Purulia, Anubhav successfully executed projects tackling malnutrition and lack of educational supplies. We reached over 200 families during our tenure here, executing development drives and health camps in remote villages.

Headquarters: The Nerve Center

Diamond Harbour is the beating heart of Anubhav—the strategic nerve center where compassion meets action. This isn't just an office; it is a community sanctuary. Here, we orchestrate life-saving blood donation camps, conduct rapid-response emergency planning, and foster civic engagement. It is the lighthouse from which every wave of our relief work originates, setting a gold standard for local community support and operational excellence.

Green Bengal: A Global Pledge

Our planet is not an inheritance from our ancestors, but a loan from our children. At Anubhav, our 'Green Bengal' initiative is a crusade against climate change. From massive reforestation drives restoring rural lung spaces to aggressive plastic-free campaigns in urban markets, we are engineering a sustainable future. We don't just plant trees; we plant hope, ensuring that the next generation breathes cleaner air and walks on greener earth.

Our Story

Anubhav began its journey during the devastating COVID-19 outbreak. When the world was in lockdown, we saw families in Namkhana and Sundarbans starving for basic needs. We launched back-to-back relief projects for 100 households, providing food, sanitary pads, and baby food. That commitment to humanity drives us today across West Bengal.

Our Projects

Where Anubhav Began

The Sundarbans isn't just a project location for us; it is the birthplace of Anubhav. Our journey began here during the height of the COVID-19 outbreak, when the region was paralyzed by lockdowns. Witnessing families in Namkhana and the deep interior struggle for basic needs, we launched our first-ever relief drives. We delivered food, sanitary pads, baby food, and masks to over 100 households.

Current Status: What started as emergency relief has evolved into a permanent Coaching Center. We now support nearly 70 children here, providing free education and nutrition.

1. Peniabhnga - Festive Joy

Peniabhnga, Hargram | Durga Puja 2024

On the auspicious eve of Durga Puja, we celebrated with the SABAR tribal group. We distributed 60 new clothes, learning kits, cakes, and biscuits to children. Collaborated with Mrs. Sapna Midya. We also arranged a community lunch for 100 people.

2. Gopiballavpur - Winter Relief

Gopiballavpur | 29th December, 2024

We carried out a challenging project in Gopiballavpur to aid tribal families during the harsh winter. We donated 60 blankets. Conducted in collaboration with the NGO "Subarnaseva" (সুবর্ণসেবা).

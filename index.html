Who We Are

Anubhav is a registered NGO based in Diamond Harbour. We have a rich history of social work across West Bengal, ensuring that education and essential resources reach the most remote corners of Jhargram, Purulia, Bankura, and the Sundarbans.

Our Projects

Current Ongoing Project

Sundarbans Coaching Center

Located in the heart of the delta, this project currently supports nearly 70 children. We provide free coaching, study materials, and nutritional support to ensure they stay in school and succeed.
